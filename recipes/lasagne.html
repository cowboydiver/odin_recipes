Lasagne Recipe

for 4 persons

Ingridients

  • 1000 g of meat
  • 1 can of pureed tomatoes (4-500 g)
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 2 Onions
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic
  • Morney Sauce
  • Lasagne plates
  • Grated cheese
  • 1 dl of Balsamico (or red wine vinegar)
  • 2 tbsp of dried basil
  • 2 tbsp of dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking oil. Olive oil prefered

How to make this thing

  1. Chop onions and garlic
  2. Add a good chunk of cooking oil to a cooking pot or sort√© pan and heat to medium-high heat
  3. Add onions and garlic and let fry for 3-4 min, but leave one clove of garlic for later
  4. Add minched meat and fry until all the meat has turned brown
  5. Add pureed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, sugar, balsamico and the last clove of garlic. Season with salt and peber
  6. Turn down heat to low and let simmer for 30-60 min without lid. The longer the better.
  7. Taste sauce and add salt, pebber and balsamico until it taste is spot on
  8. Pour some of the lasagne souce in a lasagne dish, then add some Morney sauce, then cover with lasagne plates. Repeat until out of lasagne sauce. Add grated cheese as the final layer
  9. Put in preheated oven on 200 Celsius for 30-40 min or until the chesse is golden brown
  10. Let rest for 15 min before serving

Tip: replace some of the meat with grated carrots or egg plant for a healthier and cheaper dish
